BLOCKCHAIN FOR SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT
=======================================

Blockchain technology offers promising solutions for supply chain management
by providing transparency, traceability, and immutability. 

Key benefits:
- End-to-end traceability of goods
- Smart contracts for automated payments
- Tamper-proof record keeping
- Reduction of counterfeit goods

Hyperledger Fabric vs Ethereum for supply chain:
Hyperledger Fabric offers permissioned blockchain which is more suitable
for enterprise supply chains because participants are known and vetted.
Ethereum offers public blockchain with smart contracts but has scalability
issues.

Smart contracts can automate:
- Payment releases upon delivery confirmation
- Quality assurance checks
- Regulatory compliance reporting

Case studies mentioned briefly but no detailed data included.
Some companies are using blockchain for food traceability, pharma, and
luxury goods verification.

Challenges: scalability, interoperability between different blockchain
networks, integration with legacy systems, and regulatory uncertainty.
