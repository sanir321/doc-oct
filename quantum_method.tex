\section{Methodology}
The proposed quantum neural network architecture consists of:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Encoding layer: Angle encoding of classical data
    \item Variational layers: $ layers of parameterized gates
    \item Measurement layer: Pauli-Z expectation measurements
\end{itemize}

The expressibility of a quantum circuit is defined as:
\[
\mathcal{E} = \| \int_{\mathcal{U}} U^{\otimes t} dU - \int_{\theta} U(\theta)^{\otimes t} d\theta \|_2
\]

where $\mathcal{U}$ is the Haar measure over the unitary group.
